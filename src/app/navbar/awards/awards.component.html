June, 2023 -National Conclave

Ar. Savita Agarwal invited as delegate in National Conclave for Future of Architectural Education at India International Centre , New Delhi.

SUSHANT UNIVERSITY organised a Conclave in which over 100 delegates from across the country including President and Executive Council members of CoA; Founding Trustee, Chancellor and Vice Chancellor of Sushant University; Former Member Secretary of AICTE; Chairman of IIA Norther Chapter; Deans and Heads of Architecture Institutes; Directors; Research Scholars and Faculty Members came under one roof at the National Conclave on the Future of Architecture Education in India. 

Burning issues related to the Architects Act 1972, NEP 2020, Interim report on education reforms by CoA 2023, UGC guidelines on curriculum framework, jobs and employability, salaries and remuneration, project consultancy fee, entry-exit-intake dynamics, UG-PG progression, UG and PG degrees, skills and practice etc were critically discussed in an open, transparent, stimulating and constructive manner.
