About us

Muralage is one of Lucknow's oldest and the country's leading & fast-growing Architectural & Project Management firm with branch offices in New Delhi, Ahmedabad, Indore and Patna. The firm includes Architect Partners and Associates in various allied professions. All the Partners, Associates, and staff members are qualified and experienced in their respective fields. The firm was established in 1974 and since then has been busy with various kinds of projects, a few of which have been summarized on the website. Projects are approached broadly. Solutions start with programming and research in depth. These are followed by preliminary and final drawings, quantity surveys, costing, and scheduling. Plan work then continues through working drawings and specifications. Supervision is an integral part of the process to ensure sound completion We have entered into hundreds of successful partnerships with our clients. In fact, most of our work is generated by referrals from satisfied clients. Our staff is comprised of talented, educated professionals. We are design-oriented and highly proficient in creative innovation and technical execution. Our in-house services include architectural design, urban design, engineering, interior design, landscaping, 3D modeling, project management and more.
